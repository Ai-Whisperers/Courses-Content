# FPUNA Summer 2026 - AI-Augmented Development Course
## Project Kickoff Document

**Project Duration**: 19 weeks (9 weeks prep + 10 weeks delivery)
**Start Date**: January 6, 2026
**Course Launch**: March 9, 2026
**Course End**: May 17, 2026

---

## EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

We are developing a comprehensive 10-week university summer program that teaches students to become AI-augmented developers. The program consists of:

- **1 Core Course** (3 weeks) - Universal AI development foundations
- **2 Specialization Tracks** (5 weeks each) - QA Automation OR Web Development
- **1 Capstone Project** (2 weeks) - Real-world portfolio piece

**Target Audience**: FPUNA university students (estimated 30-50 students)

**Outcome**: Students gain practical AI-assisted development skills and build a portfolio project demonstrating their capabilities.

---

## PROJECT GOALS

### Primary Goals
1. **Create Core Course** - Extract and adapt 6 universal modules from existing QA content
2. **Develop Web Dev Track** - Create 9 new modules for web development specialization
3. **Finalize QA Track** - Review and update existing 9-module QA automation track
4. **Build Support Materials** - Instructor guides, assessment rubrics, student onboarding

### Success Criteria
- ✅ All modules complete with README, EXERCISE, QUIZ by Week 8
- ✅ 100% of exercises tested and working
- ✅ Instructor guides for every module
- ✅ ≥80% student completion rate during delivery
- ✅ ≥75% student satisfaction rating

## RISK REGISTER

|------|--------|-------------|---------------------|
| Content creation delays | High | Medium | Built-in buffer time, parallel workflows |
| AI tools change/deprecate | Medium | Low | Document multiple tool options |
