## Answer Key

### Section 1: Multiple Choice

1. **B** - Enabling better targeting, segmentation, and personalization
   - *Enriched data enables smarter sales decisions.*

2. **B** - Having 3 times your quota in total pipeline value
   - *3x coverage accounts for expected win rates and deal slippage.*

3. **B** - Number of discovery calls scheduled
   - *Leading indicators predict future results; lagging indicators measure past results.*

4. **B** - You only have one contact at the account (risky)
   - *Single-threaded deals fail if that one contact leaves or loses influence.*

5. **C** - When evidence shows the original date is no longer realistic
   - *Accurate close dates enable better forecasting.*

6. **B** - The specific reasons why you lost and what you could do differently
   - *Learning from losses prevents future losses.*

7. **B** - No activity logged for 30+ days and/or past expected close date
   - *These are classic risk signals requiring attention.*

8. **B** - Commit = high confidence deals likely to close; Best case = possible but less certain
   - *Commit is dependable; best case is upside potential.*

9. **B** - Review and edit for accuracy before saving
   - *AI output requires human verification before it becomes official record.*

10. **B** - It helps identify stuck deals that may need intervention
    - *Long time in stage often indicates problems.*

### Section 2: True/False

11. **True** - AI enrichment can be wrong; verification protects data quality.

12. **False** - An executive champion is a positive signal; single-manager contact is the higher risk.

13. **False** - Analyzing losses is often more valuable than analyzing wins for improvement.

14. **True** - Honest forecasting enables better planning; optimistic forecasts cause problems.

15. **False** - Quality of activities (conversion rates, engagement) matters more than raw volume.

### Section 3: Scenario-Based

16. **B** - Flag as at-risk, reach out through different channel, and update close date
    - *This deal shows multiple risk signals requiring immediate action.*

17. **B** - Prioritize getting executive access in all deals and update qualification criteria
    - *Clear pattern should drive process improvement.*

18. **C** - Verify with a reliable source and update based on verified info
    - *AI suggestions need verification; companies grow, so old data may be wrong.*

19. **A** - Yes - 3x coverage is typically considered healthy for B2B sales
    - *3x is a common healthy benchmark accounting for win rates.*

20. **B** - Best case - commitment is there but process risk remains
    - *Verbal yes without completed procurement process can still slip.*
