# Course Syllabus: AI for Sales Teams

## Course Information

- **Course Title**: AI for Sales Teams
- **Duration**: 20-24 hours (4 weeks, part-time)
- **Format**: Self-paced online with practical exercises
- **Level**: Beginner

---

## Course Description

This course transforms sales professionals into AI-augmented revenue drivers who prospect smarter and close faster. Students learn to leverage AI for lead research, email personalization, proposal generation, call preparation, CRM enrichment, and sales forecasting. The focus is on immediate revenue impact through practical techniques. By course completion, participants will research prospects 10x faster, personalize outreach at scale, and have AI workflows ready for immediate use.

---

## Learning Outcomes

Upon successful completion, students will be able to:

1. **Research Prospects** - Use AI to gather comprehensive prospect and company intelligence
2. **Personalize at Scale** - Write personalized emails and messages efficiently with AI
3. **Generate Proposals** - Create customized proposals and quotes in minutes
4. **Prepare for Calls** - Use AI for pre-call research and objection preparation
5. **Enrich CRM Data** - Automate data entry and lead enrichment
6. **Improve Forecasting** - Leverage AI for pipeline analysis and deal scoring
7. **Handle Objections** - Prepare responses and competitive positioning
8. **Maintain Authenticity** - Use AI without losing personal connection

- Discovery question generation
