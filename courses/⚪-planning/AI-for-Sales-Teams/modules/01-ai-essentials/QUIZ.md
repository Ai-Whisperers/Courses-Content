## Answer Key

### Section 1: Multiple Choice

1. **C** - Augmenting human capabilities to increase productivity and effectiveness
   - *AI assists sellers; it doesn't replace the human elements that close deals.*

2. **C** - Researching prospects and generating personalized content at scale
   - *These are information-processing tasks where AI excels.*

3. **B** - Verify key facts and claims before using them
   - *AI can hallucinate or have outdated information; verification is essential.*

4. **B** - Start with a free general AI assistant and add specialized tools as needed
   - *Free tools are sufficient to start; add paid tools based on proven value.*

5. **C** - Using AI to research and personalize genuine outreach
   - *This is legitimate AI assistance that enhances authentic selling.*

6. **B** - Correct it before using and note the limitation
   - *Learning from AI limitations helps you improve prompts and verification.*

7. **B** - AI handles research and content while humans drive strategy and relationships
   - *The augmentation model leverages each side's strengths.*

8. **C** - 3-6x faster (60 minutes to 10-20 minutes)
   - *This is a realistic productivity gain; 10x is overly optimistic.*

9. **B** - Providing context about your role, solution, and what you need
   - *Context enables AI to give relevant, actionable responses.*

10. **B** - To reuse effective prompts and continuously improve them
    - *Prompts are assets; saving and refining them increases efficiency.*

### Section 2: True/False

11. **False** - Relationships require genuine human connection; AI can't replicate trust and rapport.

12. **True** - Free tiers provide sufficient capability to start; upgrade based on value demonstrated.

13. **True** - Honesty about AI use is part of ethical selling; don't lie when asked directly.

14. **False** - AI content requires human review for accuracy, tone, and personalization.

15. **True** - AI provides research assistance; verification by the seller is always needed.

### Section 3: Scenario-Based

16. **B** - Verify the funding news through a reliable source
    - *Using unverified "facts" damages credibility if wrong.*

17. **B** - Remove the reference if you can't verify it, or verify before sending
    - *Specific personalization must be accurate; generic alternatives are safer than wrong specifics.*

18. **B** - Share your prompts - building team capability benefits everyone
    - *Team success lifts all boats; prompts become more valuable when refined by multiple users.*

19. **B** - A free AI assistant (Claude.ai or ChatGPT)
    - *Free tools provide immediate value; paid tools should be added after proving ROI.*

20. **B** - Remove the compliance pain point and focus on the more likely ones
    - *Apply your domain expertise to filter AI suggestions.*
