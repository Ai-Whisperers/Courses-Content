## Answer Key

### Section 1: Multiple Choice

1. **B** - Researching accounts 5-10x faster while maintaining quality
   - *AI's primary value is speed and scale, not accuracy guarantees.*

2. **C** - Recent news and funding claims
   - *AI can hallucinate or have outdated info; recent claims need verification.*

3. **B** - An event that indicates increased likelihood of a purchase decision
   - *Trigger events signal change that often creates buying opportunities.*

4. **B** - Contact within 2 weeks while the opportunity is fresh
   - *Funding creates immediate needs; waiting too long misses the window.*

5. **B** - Their likely responsibilities, challenges, and success metrics
   - *Understanding their world enables relevant, personalized outreach.*

6. **B** - To define characteristics of customers most likely to buy and succeed
   - *ICP guides targeting for efficient, effective prospecting.*

7. **B** - Company recently raised funding or hired new leadership
   - *Trigger events indicate active change; demographics are static.*

8. **B** - Review sites like G2, Capterra, and TrustRadius
   - *Customer reviews provide authentic competitive intelligence.*

9. **B** - Research-based insights specific to their situation
   - *Genuine personalization demonstrates understanding of their specific context.*

10. **B** - Treat it as a hypothesis to validate through discovery
    - *AI pain points are informed guesses; discovery confirms.*

### Section 2: True/False

11. **False** - AI research always requires human verification, especially for facts used in outreach.

12. **True** - New executives in target roles have mandates for change and typically make decisions in their first 90 days.

13. **False** - Effective competitive intelligence includes strengths (to anticipate) and weaknesses (to exploit).

14. **True** - Both fit and intent matter; high-fit with low-intent requires nurturing, high-intent with low-fit may waste time.

15. **False** - Prompts should be customized for industry context, company size, and research objectives.

### Section 3: Scenario-Based

16. **B** - Verify by checking their press releases, website, or news sources
    - *Specific claims need verification before building outreach around them.*

17. **B** - Use it to form hypotheses and discovery questions
    - *AI provides informed guesses; discovery validates them.*

18. **B** - Prioritize by ICP fit and presence of trigger events
    - *Limited time should focus on highest-probability opportunities.*

19. **B** - Admit they have the feature but pivot to what customers actually say about it
    - *Honesty with evidence is more credible than avoidance or denial.*

20. **B** - Focus most time on hot leads, scheduled touches for warm, automated nurture for others
    - *Prioritize by probability; maintain pipeline with appropriate touch levels.*
