## Course Description

Demystify AI and machine learning for business leaders. This practical, non-technical workshop helps executives and managers understand AI's potential, identify opportunities in their business, and make informed decisions about AI adoption.

Perfect for business professionals who need to understand AI without the technical jargon. Learn to evaluate AI opportunities, understand ROI, and lead AI initiatives confidently.

---

## What You'll Learn

### Module 1: AI Demystified (60 min)
- What is AI/ML - cutting through the buzzwords
- Understanding different types of AI (Generative AI, Predictive AI, etc.)
- How AI actually works (simple explanations)
- Common misconceptions about AI

### Module 2: Business Value & ROI (45 min)
- Real business use cases across industries
- Calculating ROI for AI initiatives
- Quick wins vs. long-term transformation
- Cost considerations and budgeting

### Module 3: AI Tools Landscape (45 min)
- Overview of leading AI tools and platforms
- ChatGPT, Claude, Microsoft Copilot, and more
- Industry-specific AI solutions
- Build vs. buy decisions

### Module 4: Identifying Opportunities (45 min)
- Framework for spotting AI opportunities
- Which business processes benefit most from AI
- Data readiness assessment
- Creating an AI roadmap

### Module 5: Risks & Governance (30 min)
- Limitations and failures of AI
- Data privacy and security concerns
- Bias and ethical considerations
- Building responsible AI policies

### Module 6: Hands-On Session (45 min)
- Using ChatGPT for business tasks
- AI for email writing and communication
- AI for reports and presentations
- AI for research and analysis
