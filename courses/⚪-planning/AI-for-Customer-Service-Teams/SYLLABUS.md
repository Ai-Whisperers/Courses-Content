# Course Syllabus: AI for Customer Service Teams

## Course Information

- **Course Title**: AI for Customer Service Teams
- **Duration**: 32-40 hours (6-8 weeks, part-time)
- **Format**: Self-paced online with team exercises
- **Level**: Beginner to Intermediate

---

## Course Description

This course transforms customer service teams into AI-augmented support professionals who deliver faster, higher-quality customer experiences. Students learn to implement AI chatbots, automate ticket management, leverage AI-powered response suggestions, analyze customer sentiment, and improve quality assurance. The focus is on practical implementation that enhances agent capabilities without sacrificing the human touch. By course completion, teams will achieve 20%+ efficiency gains while maintaining or improving customer satisfaction.

---

## Learning Outcomes

Upon successful completion, students will be able to:

1. **Evaluate AI Tools** - Select appropriate AI solutions for customer service operations
2. **Implement Chatbots** - Design and deploy AI chatbots for first-line support
3. **Automate Tickets** - Set up intelligent ticket routing, categorization, and prioritization
4. **Enhance Responses** - Use AI-powered response suggestions while maintaining quality
5. **Analyze Sentiment** - Monitor customer emotions and identify escalation needs
6. **Support Multiple Languages** - Leverage AI for real-time translation and localization
7. **Automate QA** - Implement AI-powered quality assurance and coaching insights
8. **Measure Impact** - Track and report on AI-driven efficiency improvements

## Module Schedule

### Week 1-2: Foundations

**Module 1: AI Fundamentals for Customer Service (4 hours)**
- AI capabilities in customer experience
- Common AI applications in support
- Evaluating AI vendors and tools
- Building the business case for AI
- Customer-centric AI principles
- First AI experiment in CS

**Module 2: AI-Powered Ticket Management (5 hours)**
- Intelligent ticket routing
- Auto-tagging and categorization
- Priority prediction algorithms
- Workload balancing
- Integration with helpdesk systems
- Practice: Set up auto-categorization

### Week 3-4: Agent Enhancement

**Module 3: Agent Assistance Tools (5 hours)**
- AI-suggested responses
- Real-time knowledge lookup
- Customer history summarization
- Response templates with AI
- Maintaining personal touch
- Practice: Generate and refine responses

**Module 4: Chatbots & Self-Service (6 hours)**
- Chatbot design principles
- Conversation flow design
- Escalation handling and handoffs
- Self-service optimization
- Bot personality and brand voice
- Practice: Configure a support chatbot

### Week 5-6: Quality & Intelligence

**Module 5: AI-Powered Quality Assurance (4 hours)**
- Automated QA scoring
- Conversation analytics
- Coaching insights extraction
- Compliance monitoring
- Performance pattern detection
- Practice: Set up QA automation

**Module 6: Sentiment Analysis & Customer Intelligence (4 hours)**
- Understanding sentiment detection
- Real-time sentiment monitoring
- Escalation triggers
- Customer emotion tracking
- Voice of customer analysis
- Practice: Analyze customer interactions

### Week 7-8: Advanced & Implementation

**Module 7: Knowledge Management & Multi-Language (4 hours)**
- AI-enhanced knowledge bases
- Content gap identification
- Automatic article updates
- Multi-language support setup
- Translation quality assurance
- Cultural considerations

**Module 8: Implementation & Change Management (4 hours)**
- Building team buy-in
- Phased rollout strategies
- Managing agent concerns about AI
- Measuring success metrics
- Continuous improvement
- ROI reporting

**Module 9: Final Project - AI Transformation Plan (4 hours)**
- Current state assessment
- AI opportunity identification
- Implementation roadmap
- ROI projection
- Team adoption plan
- Final presentation
