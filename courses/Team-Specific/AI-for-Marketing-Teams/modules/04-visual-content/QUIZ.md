### Multiple Choice
1. **B** - Subject + Style + Mood/Lighting + Composition + Technical Details is the recommended framework.
2. **C** - Canva AI has the lowest learning curve and includes brand kit features.
3. **B** - A comprehensive visual style guide includes color palette, image style, composition preferences, and consistent elements.
4. **C** - Meta requires disclosure for realistic AI images in ads; other platforms have varying policies.

### True/False
5. **False** - In the US, pure AI-generated images without significant human creative input are generally not copyrightable.
6. **True** - Visual style guides ensure consistency across AI-generated visuals.
7. **False** - Blog headers often work best without text or with minimal design-integrated text.
8. **False** - Generating images of real people without consent raises ethical and legal concerns.

### Scenario-Based
9. **B** - Adapt visuals for each platform's norms while maintaining brand consistency.
10. **C** - Regenerate with explicit color requirements or manually adjust to match brand colors.
