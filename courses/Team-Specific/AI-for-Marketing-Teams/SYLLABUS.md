# Course Syllabus: AI for Marketing Teams

## Course Information

- **Course Title**: AI for Marketing Teams
- **Duration**: 28-36 hours (5-7 weeks, part-time)
- **Format**: Self-paced online with hands-on projects
- **Level**: Intermediate

---

## Course Description

This course transforms marketing professionals into AI-augmented content creators and strategists. Students learn to leverage AI tools for content creation, SEO optimization, visual design, analytics, personalization, and workflow automation. The focus is on practical application while maintaining brand authenticity and quality standards. By course completion, marketers will produce 2-3x more content with consistent quality and have a complete AI marketing strategy ready for implementation.

---

## Learning Outcomes

Upon successful completion, students will be able to:

1. **Evaluate AI Tools** - Select appropriate AI tools for specific marketing tasks and understand their limitations
2. **Create Content at Scale** - Generate blog posts, social media content, and email copy with AI assistance
3. **Maintain Brand Voice** - Develop and apply brand voice guidelines to ensure AI content consistency
4. **Optimize for Search** - Use AI for keyword research, content optimization, and competitive analysis
5. **Design Visual Content** - Create marketing visuals using AI image generation and design tools
6. **Analyze Performance** - Extract actionable insights from marketing data using AI analysis
7. **Personalize at Scale** - Implement AI-driven audience segmentation and content personalization
8. **Automate Workflows** - Build AI-enhanced marketing automation sequences
9. **Quality Control** - Critically evaluate AI outputs and implement review processes

## Course Policies

### Completion Requirements
- Complete all module exercises
- Pass all quizzes (70% minimum)
- Submit portfolio pieces
- Complete final project
- Achieve overall score of 70%+

### AI Usage Policy
- AI assistance is required and encouraged
- All AI usage must be documented
- You must understand and be able to explain AI-generated content
- Final outputs must be reviewed and edited by you
- Brand voice consistency is your responsibility

### Academic Integrity
- All work must demonstrate your editorial judgment
- Copying AI outputs without review/editing is insufficient
- Plagiarism of others' work results in course failure
- AI interaction logs must be submitted with exercises

### Late Submissions
- Exercises: 10% penalty per day late (max 5 days)
- Portfolio pieces: 10% penalty per day late
- Final Project: Must be submitted by deadline

## Success Tips

1. **Practice Daily** - Use AI tools in your actual work immediately
2. **Iterate** - Your first AI output is never final; refine continuously
3. **Document Everything** - Track what prompts work for your brand
4. **Stay Critical** - AI makes mistakes; always review outputs
5. **Experiment** - Try different approaches to find what works
6. **Build Your Prompt Library** - Save effective prompts for reuse
7. **Focus on Brand Voice** - This is your competitive advantage over generic AI
