## Design Notes

### Color Coding
- **Blue backgrounds**: Concept/theory slides
- **Green backgrounds**: Exercise/practice slides
- **Yellow accents**: Tips and warnings
- **Gray**: Supporting information

### Typography Rules
- Titles: 36-44pt bold
- Headers: 28-32pt bold
- Body: 20-24pt regular
- Captions: 16-18pt italic

### Visual Balance
- Maximum 6 lines of text
- 40% text, 60% visual space
- Consistent margins
- Aligned elements
