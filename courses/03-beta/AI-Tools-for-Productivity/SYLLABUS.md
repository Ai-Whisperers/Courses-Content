# Course Syllabus: AI Tools for Productivity

## Course Information

- **Course Title**: AI Tools for Productivity
- **Duration**: 24-32 hours (4-6 weeks, part-time)
- **Format**: Self-paced online with hands-on exercises
- **Level**: Beginner to Intermediate

---

## Course Description

This course transforms business professionals into AI-augmented knowledge workers. Students learn to leverage ChatGPT, Claude, and other AI tools for document creation, data analysis, presentations, meeting productivity, research, and workflow automation. The focus is on practical daily applications that deliver immediate time savings. By course completion, participants will save 5-10 hours per week and have personalized AI workflows ready for implementation.

---

## Learning Outcomes

Upon successful completion, students will be able to:

1. **Select AI Tools** - Choose appropriate AI tools for specific business tasks
2. **Create Documents** - Generate requirements, PRDs, reports, and emails with AI assistance
3. **Analyze Data** - Extract insights from spreadsheets and documents using AI
4. **Build Presentations** - Create compelling slide decks and talking points
5. **Optimize Meetings** - Leverage AI for preparation, notes, and follow-ups
6. **Conduct Research** - Synthesize information and competitive intelligence
7. **Automate Workflows** - Build simple AI-enhanced productivity systems
8. **Quality Control** - Critically evaluate AI outputs and avoid common errors
9. **Work Securely** - Use AI tools while maintaining data privacy

## Module Schedule

### Week 1: Foundations

**Module 1: AI Fundamentals for Business Professionals (3 hours)**
- What AI can and cannot do
- Choosing the right tool (ChatGPT, Claude, Copilot)
- Understanding AI limitations
- Ethics and appropriate use
- Setting up your AI toolkit
- First AI productivity experiment

### Week 2: Document Creation

**Module 2: Document Generation & Enhancement (4 hours)**
- Requirements documents
- PRDs and user stories
- Business reports and proposals
- Executive summaries
- Email and communication
- Editing and proofreading with AI
- Maintaining professional tone

### Week 3: Meetings & Communication

**Module 3: Meeting & Communication Productivity (4 hours)**
- Meeting preparation and agendas
- Note-taking and summarization
- Action item extraction
- Stakeholder updates
- Presentation creation
- Follow-up automation
- Multi-channel communication

### Week 4: Data & Analysis

**Module 4: Data Analysis & Insights (5 hours)**
- Analyzing data with AI
- Spreadsheet formula assistance
- Creating reports and visualizations
- Extracting insights from documents
- Competitive analysis
- Trend identification
- Communicating data findings

### Week 5: Research & Automation

**Module 5: Research & Information Synthesis (4 hours)**
- Market research techniques
- User research synthesis
- Competitive intelligence
- Trend analysis
- Document summarization
- Knowledge management

**Module 6: Workflow Automation (4 hours)**
- Identifying automation opportunities
- Building simple AI workflows
- Integration with existing tools
- Template creation
- Measuring productivity gains
- Scaling successful patterns

### Week 6: Quality & Final Project

**Module 7: Critical Evaluation & Security (3 hours)**
- Verifying AI outputs
- Avoiding hallucinations
- Maintaining professional judgment
- Building review processes
- Data privacy best practices
- Company policy compliance

**Module 8: Final Project - Productivity Transformation (5 hours)**
- Personal workflow audit
- AI integration plan
- Implementation of top workflows
- Results measurement
- Team sharing presentation

## Success Tips

1. **Start Small** - Pick one workflow to transform first
2. **Practice Daily** - Use AI tools in your actual work immediately
3. **Iterate** - Your first AI output is never final; refine continuously
4. **Stay Critical** - AI makes mistakes; always review outputs
5. **Build Templates** - Save effective prompts for reuse
6. **Measure Impact** - Track your time savings
7. **Share Wins** - Help colleagues adopt successful patterns
