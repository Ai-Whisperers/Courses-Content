## Learning Outcomes

By completing this course, you will be able to:

1. **Select AI Tools** - Choose appropriate AI tools for specific business tasks
2. **Create Documents** - Generate requirements, PRDs, reports, and emails with AI
3. **Analyze Data** - Extract insights from spreadsheets and documents
4. **Build Presentations** - Create compelling slide decks and talking points
5. **Optimize Meetings** - Leverage AI for preparation, notes, and follow-ups
6. **Conduct Research** - Synthesize information and competitive intelligence
7. **Automate Workflows** - Build simple AI-enhanced productivity systems
8. **Quality Control** - Critically evaluate AI outputs and avoid common errors
