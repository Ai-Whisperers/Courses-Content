## Design Guidelines

### Safety Factors
- Structure: 1.5-2.0
- Propulsion: 1.3
- Battery: 20% reserve

### Regulations
- DINAC (Paraguay aviation authority)
- Maximum takeoff weight
- Operating altitude limits
- Line of sight requirements

### Best Practices
- Keep it simple (KISS)
- Design for manufacturing
- Test critical components
- Document everything
