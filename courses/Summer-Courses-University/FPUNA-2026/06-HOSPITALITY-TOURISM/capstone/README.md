# Capstone: Plan de Negocio Turístico Paraguay
## Track 06 - Hospitality & Tourism

**Duración**: 20 horas  
**Peso**: 50% de calificación final

---

## Descripción

Crear plan completo de negocio turístico para Paraguay usando AI en todos los aspectos.

---

## Componentes Requeridos

1. **Business Concept**
   - Producto/servicio turístico
   - Target market
   - Value proposition
   - Paraguay focus

2. **AI Customer Service**
   - Chatbot implementation
   - Multi-channel support
   - 24/7 availability

3. **Revenue Management**
   - Pricing strategy
   - Demand forecasting
   - Yield optimization

4. **Digital Marketing**
   - Social media strategy
   - Content marketing
   - SEO/SEM
   - Influencer partnerships

5. **Operations Plan**
   - Process automation
   - Staff management
   - Quality control
   - AI optimization

6. **Financial Projections**
   - Startup costs
   - Revenue forecast (3 years)
   - Break-even analysis
   - ROI calculations
