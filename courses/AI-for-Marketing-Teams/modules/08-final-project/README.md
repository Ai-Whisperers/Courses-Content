# Module 8: Final Project - AI Marketing Strategy

**Duration**: 5 hours
**Prerequisites**: Modules 1-7

---

## Learning Objectives

By the end of this module, you will:

1. Synthesize all course learnings into a comprehensive strategy
2. Create a complete AI marketing implementation plan
3. Design workflows for your specific context
4. Build a measurement framework for AI marketing
5. Present your strategy professionally

---

## Project Overview

### What You'll Create

A comprehensive **AI Marketing Strategy Document** that includes:

1. **Current State Assessment** - Where you are today
2. **AI Tool Selection** - What tools you'll use and why
3. **Content Strategy** - AI-enhanced content plan
4. **Implementation Roadmap** - Phased rollout plan
5. **Workflow Designs** - Specific automated workflows
6. **Measurement Framework** - How you'll track success
7. **Team Adoption Plan** - How you'll bring others along

### Deliverables

- AI Marketing Strategy Document (15-25 pages)
- Prompt Library (minimum 20 prompts)
- Sample AI-Generated Content Portfolio (10+ pieces)
- 10-minute Video Presentation

## Grading Rubric

| Component | Weight | Criteria |
|-----------|--------|----------|
| Strategy Document Quality | 30% | Comprehensive, actionable, well-organized |
| Implementation Realism | 20% | Achievable, well-planned, practical |
| Content Portfolio | 20% | Quality, variety, brand consistency |
| Prompt Library | 15% | Useful, well-documented, effective |
| Presentation | 15% | Clear, professional, compelling |

### Grading Scale
- A (90-100%): Exceptional strategy ready for implementation
- B (80-89%): Strong strategy with minor gaps
- C (70-79%): Adequate strategy needing refinement
- F (<70%): Incomplete or impractical strategy

---

## Tips for Success

1. **Be Specific** - Generic strategies don't work; tailor to your context
2. **Be Realistic** - A simple plan you'll execute beats a complex one you won't
3. **Show Your Work** - Include AI interaction examples and iterations
4. **Measure Everything** - If you can't measure it, you can't improve it
5. **Think Long-term** - This is a foundation for ongoing AI usage
