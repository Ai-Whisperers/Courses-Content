## Part 1: Create Your Brand Context Document (~20 minutes)

### Task

Create a comprehensive brand context document that you'll use throughout this course to maintain consistency in AI-generated content.

### Steps

1. Open a new document (Google Docs, Notion, or plain text)

2. Fill in the following template:

```markdown
# Brand Context for AI

## Company Overview
Company Name: [Your company or a fictional one]
Industry: [Industry]
What we do: [One sentence description]
Target audience: [Who you serve]
Unique value: [What makes you different]

## Brand Voice
Primary tone: [Choose: Professional / Conversational / Playful / Authoritative / Empathetic]

We sound like: [Describe in 3-5 adjectives]
Example: "Knowledgeable but approachable, like a trusted advisor who explains complex topics simply"

Words we use:
- [Word 1]
- [Word 2]
- [Word 3]

Words we NEVER use:
- [Word 1]
- [Word 2]
- [Word 3]

## Target Audience Profile
Demographics:
- Age range: [X-X]
- Job titles: [List 3-5]
- Industry: [Industry]

Psychographics:
- Main challenge: [Biggest pain point]
- Goal: [What they want to achieve]
- How they consume content: [Channels, format preferences]

## Content Style Guidelines
- Sentence length: [Short/Medium/Long or word count]
- Paragraph length: [X sentences]
- Use of jargon: [Avoid / Use sparingly / OK for this audience]
- Formatting: [Lists preferred / Long paragraphs OK / Mix]
- CTAs: [Direct / Soft / Question-based]

## Sample Content (Our Best Voice)
[Paste 1-2 paragraphs from your best existing content
that represents how you want to sound]
```

3. Save this document - you'll use it throughout the course

### Deliverable

- [ ] Completed brand context document (minimum 200 words)

## Part 2: First AI Content Creation (~30 minutes)

### Task

Use your brand context to generate a LinkedIn post with AI, then apply the REVIEW framework.

### Steps

1. Open Claude.ai (or your chosen AI tool)

2. Use this prompt template:

```
You are helping me create marketing content for my brand.

[PASTE YOUR ENTIRE BRAND CONTEXT DOCUMENT HERE]

---

TASK: Write a LinkedIn post about [choose a topic relevant to your industry].

REQUIREMENTS:
- Length: 150-250 words
- Include a hook in the first line
- Share a specific insight or tip
- End with a question to encourage engagement
- Professional but conversational tone
- No hashtags in the body (add 3 at the end)
```

3. Generate the content

4. Apply the REVIEW framework:

**R - Read completely**: Read the entire post out loud

**E - Evaluate accuracy**:
- Are any claims made that need verification?
- Any statistics or data points to check?

**V - Verify brand voice**:
- Does it match your brand document?
- Circle any words that feel "off-brand"

**W - Watch for AI tells**: Highlight and remove:
- Generic phrases ("In today's fast-paced...")
- Overused structures
- Clichés

**I - Improve clarity**:
- Simplify complex sentences
- Remove unnecessary words

**E - Enhance with expertise**:
- Add a personal example or story
- Include your unique perspective

5. Create your edited final version

### Deliverable

- [ ] Original AI output (copy/paste the raw response)
- [ ] Annotated version showing REVIEW edits
- [ ] Final edited post ready for publishing

## Part 3: Visual Content Test (~20 minutes)

### Task

Create a simple graphic in Canva using AI features.

### Steps

1. Go to Canva.com and log in

2. Click "Create a design" → "Instagram Post" (1080x1080)

3. Try Canva's AI features:
   - Click "Magic Write" in the text tool to generate copy
   - Try "Magic Design" to generate layout suggestions
   - Explore "Text to Image" if available on your plan

4. Create a simple graphic that:
   - Promotes your LinkedIn post topic
   - Uses your brand colors (set them up in Brand Kit if available)
   - Includes a clear headline
   - Has a call-to-action

5. Export and save

### Deliverable

- [ ] Completed Canva graphic (screenshot or exported image)
- [ ] Notes on which AI features you used

4. How do you think AI will change your content workflow?

## Example: REVIEW Process in Action

**Original AI Output:**
> In today's fast-paced business world, marketing teams are constantly looking for ways to improve their efficiency. AI tools have emerged as a game-changer, offering unprecedented opportunities to streamline workflows and boost productivity. Here are three ways AI can help your marketing team work smarter, not harder...

**After REVIEW:**
> Last month, our team cut blog production time by 60% - without hiring anyone new. The secret? We stopped treating AI as a magic button and started using it as a thinking partner. Here's what actually worked (and what flopped):
>
> [Specific examples from your experience]
>
> What's your team's biggest AI win so far?

**What Changed:**
- ❌ "In today's fast-paced world" → ✅ Specific result + timeframe
- ❌ Generic promise → ✅ Authentic experience
- ❌ "Game-changer" cliché → ✅ Concrete metaphor
- ❌ Listicle preview → ✅ Story hook with vulnerability
