# Module 7 Exercise: Automation Workflow Design

**Duration**: 2 hours
**Difficulty**: Intermediate

---

## Objective

Design and document a complete marketing automation system with AI enhancement points.

---

## Part 1: Workflow Audit (~25 min)

### Task
Audit your current marketing workflows for automation opportunities.

### Deliverables
- [ ] List of 10+ marketing tasks with time estimates
- [ ] Automation potential rating for each
- [ ] Top 5 priorities ranked by ROI potential

---

## Part 2: Automation Sequence Design (~40 min)

### Task
Design one complete automation sequence.

Choose one:
- Welcome sequence (new subscribers)
- Lead nurture sequence (new leads)
- Re-engagement sequence (inactive contacts)

### Deliverables
- [ ] Complete sequence map (5-7 emails)
- [ ] Trigger conditions documented
- [ ] Branching logic defined
- [ ] AI enhancement points identified

---

## Part 3: Lead Scoring Model (~30 min)

### Task
Create a lead scoring model for your business.

### Deliverables
- [ ] Scoring criteria with point values
- [ ] Threshold definitions (Cold, Warm, MQL, SQL)
- [ ] Alert triggers documented

---

## Part 4: ROI Projection (~25 min)

### Task
Calculate expected ROI for your automation.

### Deliverables
- [ ] Time savings calculation
- [ ] Quality improvement estimate
- [ ] ROI projection and payback period

---

## Grading Rubric

| Criterion | Weight |
|-----------|--------|
| Workflow Audit Quality | 25% |
| Automation Sequence Design | 35% |
| Lead Scoring Model | 25% |
| ROI Analysis | 15% |
