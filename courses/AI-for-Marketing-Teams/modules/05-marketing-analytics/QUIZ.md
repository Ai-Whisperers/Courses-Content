## Section 3: Scenario-Based (3 points each)

### Question 9

**Scenario:**
Your AI analysis shows that email marketing has a 400% higher ROI than paid social. However, email only accounts for 5% of your total leads.

**What's the best interpretation?**

A) Immediately shift all budget from paid social to email
B) Email is more efficient but may not scale; test increasing email investment gradually while maintaining reach through paid social
C) The data must be wrong since paid social gets more engagement
D) Focus only on ROI and eliminate paid social entirely

### Question 10

**How should you proceed?**

A) Immediately change all publishing to Tuesdays
B) Acknowledge the insight but recognize the small sample size; run a controlled test with more Tuesday posts before changing strategy
C) Ignore the finding since AI can't understand publishing
D) Only publish on Tuesdays going forward without testing

## Answer Key (Instructor Only)

### Multiple Choice
1. **B** - Collect, Clean, Analyze, Interpret, Visualize, Action is the correct workflow order.
2. **B** - Clean formatting, clear headers, and consistent structure are essential for AI analysis.
3. **B** - Executive summaries should lead with business impact and key headlines.
4. **C** - Always cross-check AI insights with source data and apply domain knowledge.

### True/False
5. **False** - AI augments but doesn't replace human judgment in analytics.
6. **True** - Forecasts should include scenario ranges to account for uncertainty.
7. **True** - The framework transforms raw insights into actionable narratives.
8. **False** - Anomalies should be investigated; they may reveal important insights or issues.

### Scenario-Based
9. **B** - Consider both efficiency AND scale. Email may not scale infinitely, so gradual testing while maintaining reach is prudent.
10. **B** - Small sample sizes require validation. Test the hypothesis with more data before changing strategy.
