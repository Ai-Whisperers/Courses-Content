# Module 6 Exercise: Personalization Strategy

**Duration**: 2 hours
**Difficulty**: Intermediate

---

## Objective

Create a complete personalization strategy including audience segments, personalized content, and A/B test plans.

---

## Part 1: Audience Segmentation (~30 min)

### Task
Create 4 distinct audience segments for your brand.

### Deliverables
- [ ] 4 detailed segment profiles
- [ ] Segment comparison matrix
- [ ] Priority ranking with rationale

---

## Part 2: Content Personalization (~35 min)

### Task
Create personalized content variations for your top 2 segments.

### Deliverables
- [ ] Email subject line variations (5 per segment)
- [ ] Value proposition variations (3 per segment)
- [ ] CTA variations (4 per segment)

---

## Part 3: Personalized Email Sequence (~35 min)

### Task
Design a 3-email nurture sequence personalized for one segment.

### Deliverables
- [ ] 3-email sequence with segment-specific content
- [ ] Subject lines for each email
- [ ] Branching logic documentation

---

## Part 4: A/B Test Plan (~20 min)

### Task
Create 2 A/B test hypotheses for your personalized content.

### Deliverables
- [ ] 2 test hypotheses with expected impact
- [ ] Success metrics defined
- [ ] Sample size requirements

---

## Grading Rubric

| Criterion | Weight |
|-----------|--------|
| Segment Quality | 30% |
| Personalization Depth | 30% |
| Email Sequence | 25% |
| A/B Test Planning | 15% |
