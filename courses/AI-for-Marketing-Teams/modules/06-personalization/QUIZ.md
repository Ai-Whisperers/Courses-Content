## Section 3: Scenario (3 points)

### Question 7
You have two segments: "Enterprise Decision Makers" and "Startup Founders." How should your landing page headline differ?

A) Use the same headline for both - consistency matters
B) Enterprise: Focus on ROI and risk reduction; Startup: Focus on speed and growth
C) Only change the company logo
D) Make both headlines longer

---

## Answer Key

<details>
<summary>Answers</summary>

1. **B** - Demographic, Behavioral, Psychographic
2. **B** - Benefits and proof points relevant to that segment
3. **B** - A prediction about what will improve results and why
4. **True**
5. **False** - Sequences should be personalized by segment
6. **True**
7. **B** - Different segments have different priorities
