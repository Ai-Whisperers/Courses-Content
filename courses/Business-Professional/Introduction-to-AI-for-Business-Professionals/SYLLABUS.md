# Course Syllabus: Introduction to AI for Business Professionals

## Course Information

- **Course Title**: Introduction to AI for Business Professionals
- **Duration**: 12-16 hours (2-3 weeks, part-time)
- **Format**: Self-paced online with hands-on exercises
- **Level**: Beginner (No technical background required)

---

## Course Description

This course demystifies AI and machine learning for business leaders, executives, and non-technical professionals. Students learn to understand AI capabilities, identify opportunities in their organization, calculate potential ROI, evaluate vendors, and use AI tools productively in daily work. The focus is on practical business applications without technical jargon. By course completion, participants will confidently identify 3-5 AI opportunities in their business and use AI tools effectively.

---

## Learning Outcomes

Upon successful completion, students will be able to:

1. **Explain AI Concepts** - Describe AI/ML fundamentals without technical jargon
2. **Identify Opportunities** - Spot 3-5 AI opportunities in their organization
3. **Calculate ROI** - Estimate potential returns for AI initiatives
4. **Evaluate Vendors** - Assess AI solutions and providers effectively
5. **Manage Risks** - Understand AI limitations, ethics, and governance needs
6. **Use AI Tools** - Apply ChatGPT, Claude, and similar tools productively
7. **Lead AI Initiatives** - Champion responsible AI adoption in their teams
8. **Make Informed Decisions** - Separate AI hype from practical reality

---

## Module Schedule

### Week 1: Understanding AI

**Module 1: AI Demystified (3 hours)**
- What is AI/ML - cutting through buzzwords
- Types of AI: narrow vs. general, generative vs. predictive
- How AI actually works (simple explanations)
- What AI can and cannot do today
- Common misconceptions debunked
- Real-world examples across industries

**Module 2: Business Value & ROI (3 hours)**
- AI success stories across industries
- Calculating ROI for AI initiatives
- Quick wins vs. long-term transformation
- Cost considerations and budgeting
- Build vs. buy decisions
- AI tools landscape overview

### Week 2: Opportunities & Risks

**Module 3: Identifying Opportunities (3 hours)**
- Framework for spotting AI opportunities
- Which business processes benefit most
- Data readiness assessment
- Prioritization criteria
- Starting your opportunity list
- Workshop: Map AI opportunities in your organization

**Module 4: Managing Risk & Governance (3 hours)**
- Limitations and failures of AI
- Bias and fairness concerns
- Privacy and security risks
- Regulatory landscape overview
- Governance frameworks
- Building responsible AI practices

### Week 3: Hands-On & Action

**Module 5: Hands-On with AI Tools (3 hours)**
- Setting up ChatGPT and Claude
- AI for email writing and communication
- AI for reports and presentations
- AI for research and analysis
- Prompt basics for better results
- Security and privacy best practices

**Module 6: Final Project - AI Opportunity Assessment (2 hours)**
- Document your AI opportunities
- Prioritize by ROI and feasibility
- Create action plan
- Identify next steps
- Build your business case
