# Introduction to AI for Business Professionals - Templates

> Ready-to-use templates for common business tasks

---

## Email Templates

### Request for Information

```
Subject: Request: [Topic] Information Needed by [Date]

Hi [Name],

I'm working on [project/task] and need your help with [specific information needed].

Specifically, I'm looking for:
1. [Item 1]
2. [Item 2]
3. [Item 3]

Could you please provide this by [date]? If that timeline doesn't work, let me know what's realistic.

If you have questions or need clarification, I'm happy to discuss.

Thanks,
[Your name]
```

---

### Project Update

```
Subject: [Project Name] Update - Week of [Date]

Hi [Team/Stakeholder],

Here's this week's update on [Project Name]:

**Progress:**
- Completed: [accomplishment 1]
- Completed: [accomplishment 2]
- In progress: [current work]

**Upcoming:**
- [Next milestone] - Due [date]
- [Following milestone] - Due [date]

**Risks/Blockers:**
- [Any issues, or "None at this time"]

**Need From You:**
- [Specific request, or "No action needed"]

Let me know if you have questions.

[Your name]
```

### Meeting Follow-Up

```
Subject: Follow-up: [Meeting Topic] - [Date]

Hi [Attendees],

Thanks for the productive discussion on [topic]. Here's a summary:

**Key Decisions:**
- [Decision 1]
- [Decision 2]

**Action Items:**
| Task | Owner | Due Date |
|------|-------|----------|
| [Task 1] | [Name] | [Date] |
| [Task 2] | [Name] | [Date] |

**Next Steps:**
- [Next meeting or milestone]

Please reply if I missed anything or if you have updates.

Best,
[Your name]
```

## Meeting Templates

### Meeting Agenda

```
# Meeting: [Title]

**Date:** [Date and Time]
**Duration:** [Length]
**Attendees:** [Names]
**Location/Link:** [Where]

## Purpose
[One sentence describing why we're meeting]

## Agenda

| Time | Topic | Lead | Notes |
|------|-------|------|-------|
| 5 min | Welcome & Context | [Name] | Set the stage |
| 15 min | [Main Topic 1] | [Name] | [Goal for this section] |
| 15 min | [Main Topic 2] | [Name] | [Goal for this section] |
| 10 min | Discussion | All | Open floor |
| 5 min | Next Steps | [Name] | Assign actions |

## Pre-Work (if any)
- [What to review before meeting]

## Desired Outcomes
- [ ] [Outcome 1]
- [ ] [Outcome 2]
```

2. **[Finding 2]:** [Brief explanation and impact]

- [Immediate action]
- [Near-term action]
- [Long-term consideration]

See full report for: [what's in the detailed version]

## Presentation Templates

```

- [Your name and date]
- [Company/context]

1. [Topic 1]
2. [Topic 2]
3. [Topic 3]
4. Discussion/Q&A

- What problem are we solving?
- Why does it matter?
- What's at stake?

## Slides 4-6: Main Content
- [Key point 1 with supporting data]
- [Key point 2 with supporting data]
- [Key point 3 with supporting data]

## Slide 7: Recommendation
- What we should do
- Expected outcomes
- Required resources

## Slide 8: Next Steps
- [Immediate action]
- [Who does what]
- [Timeline]

## Slide 9: Discussion
- Questions?
- Contact information
```

## AI Prompt Templates

### Email Drafting

```
Help me write an email.

To: [Recipient and their role]
From: [Your role]
Purpose: [What you want to achieve]
Tone: [Professional/casual/urgent]
Key points:
1. [Point 1]
2. [Point 2]
3. [Point 3]
Length: [Short/medium/detailed]
Call to action: [What you want them to do]
```

### Meeting Preparation

```
Help me prepare for a meeting.

Topic: [What the meeting is about]
My role: [What I'm presenting/contributing]
Attendees: [Who will be there and their interests]
Duration: [How long]
Goal: [What success looks like]

I need:
1. Key talking points
2. Questions I might be asked
3. Data/examples to prepare
4. Potential objections and responses
```

### Document Analysis

```
Analyze this document and provide:

1. Summary (3-5 sentences)
2. Key points (bulleted list)
3. Action items for me
4. Questions I should ask
5. Potential concerns

[Paste document or describe it]

Focus on: [specific aspect if any]
My role: [your perspective for analysis]
```
