## Answer Key

### Section 1: Multiple Choice

1. **B** - Organization context and key findings
   - *Explanation: Executive summaries orient readers with context before details.*

2. **B** - The current state pain points and estimated business value
   - *Explanation: Business value justifies investment; pain points show need.*

3. **B** - The opportunity scores high across all criteria
   - *Explanation: VALUE total of 25 means perfect scores on all five dimensions.*

4. **B** - Foundation activities: stakeholder alignment, requirements, vendor evaluation
   - *Explanation: Early phases should establish foundations before implementation.*

5. **B** - Quantified value, realistic costs, clear ROI, and risk mitigation
   - *Explanation: Leadership needs business justification with credible numbers.*

6. **C** - Challenging (needs work before pursuing)
   - *Explanation: High value but low feasibility means blockers must be addressed first.*

7. **B** - Specific, measurable, with baseline and target
   - *Explanation: SMART metrics enable tracking progress and demonstrating success.*

8. **B** - Likelihood
   - *Explanation: Risk scores typically use L (Likelihood) × I (Impact).*

9. **B** - To build support, manage concerns, and ensure adoption
   - *Explanation: Stakeholder buy-in is critical for AI initiative success.*

10. **B** - Take immediate action on quick wins while planning strategic opportunities
    - *Explanation: Assessments should lead to action, not sit on shelves.*

### Section 2: True/False

11. **True** - Writing the summary last ensures it accurately reflects the complete analysis.

12. **False** - Business cases should include risks and mitigation to be credible.

13. **True** - Data readiness is fundamental to AI project feasibility.

14. **False** - Prioritization exists specifically to focus resources on the best opportunities.

15. **True** - Credibility requires realistic projections; inflated numbers damage trust.

### Section 3: Scenario-Based

16. **B** - Leadership support is lacking and needs attention
    - *Explanation: L=2 is the lowest score; without leadership support, projects struggle.*

17. **A** - 50%
    - *Calculation: ROI = (Value - Cost) / Cost × 100 = ($150K - $100K) / $100K × 100 = 50%*

18. **B** - Front-loaded with too much in first phase; may not be realistic
    - *Explanation: 15 tasks in 30 days is aggressive; plans should be achievable.*

19. **B** - Condense to 1 page focused on key findings, recommendations, and impact
    - *Explanation: Executive summaries should be brief; details go in other sections.*

20. **B** - Schedule meeting with leadership sponsor to present and get approval
    - *Explanation: Strong business cases deserve immediate advocacy.*
