## Part 3: First AI Experiment (30 points)

### Task
Conduct an AI-assisted response generation experiment using ChatGPT or Claude.

### Instructions

**Step 1: Setup (5 points)**

Create a prompt template for generating customer service responses. Your template should include:
- Company context
- Brand voice guidelines
- Response requirements
- Length constraints

Example structure:
```
You are a customer service agent for [describe company/industry].

Brand voice: [describe tone - friendly, professional, etc.]

Customer message:
[PASTE TICKET HERE]

Additional context:
- Product: [if applicable]
- Customer tier: [if known]

Generate a response that:
1. [Requirement 1]
2. [Requirement 2]
3. [Requirement 3]

Keep the response under [X] words.
```

Document your final prompt template.

**Step 2: Test with Sample Tickets (15 points)**

Use your prompt template to generate responses for these 5 sample tickets:

**Ticket 1: Billing Question**
> "I was charged twice for my subscription this month. Can you help me get a refund? I've been a customer for 3 years and this has never happened before."

**Ticket 2: Technical Issue**
> "The app keeps crashing every time I try to upload a photo. I've tried restarting my phone but it still doesn't work. This is really frustrating."

**Ticket 3: Feature Request**
> "It would be really helpful if you added dark mode to the app. My eyes get tired looking at the bright screen at night. Is this something you're considering?"

**Ticket 4: Angry Customer**
> "THIS IS RIDICULOUS! I've been waiting 5 days for my order and tracking shows it hasn't even shipped! I paid for express shipping! I want a full refund NOW!!!"

**Ticket 5: Complex Issue**
> "I need to transfer my account to my business partner who is taking over the company. We need all the history and data to transfer too. My partner is in a different country - is that a problem?"

For each ticket, document:
- The AI-generated response
- Your edited/final response
- What you changed and why
- Time taken (generation + editing)
- Quality rating (1-5)

**Step 3: Analysis (10 points)**

Write a 300-400 word analysis addressing:
1. How accurate were the AI responses out-of-the-box?
2. What patterns did you notice in necessary edits?
3. What types of tickets worked best with AI assistance?
4. What would you need to improve in your prompt template?
5. Do you see potential time savings? Estimate the percentage.

### Deliverable
- Prompt template
- 5 ticket responses with editing documentation
- Analysis narrative

### Grading Criteria
- Prompt template quality (5 points)
- Response documentation completeness (8 points)
- Edit quality and justifications (7 points)
- Analysis depth and insights (7 points)
- Time tracking accuracy (3 points)
