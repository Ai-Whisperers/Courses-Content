## üí° Tips for Success in This Intensive Format

### Before the Course:
1. **Clear Your Calendar** - 4 Sundays are sacred
2. **Set Up Workspace** - Comfortable setup for long sessions
3. **Communicate** - Let family/friends know your schedule
4. **Prepare Mentally** - Intensive but rewarding

### During Each Sunday:
1. **Be Present** - Minimize distractions
2. **Ask Questions** - No question is too small
3. **Take Breaks** - Use them to rest, not to work
4. **Take Notes** - You'll reference them later
5. **Participate** - Engage in activities and discussions

### Between Sundays:
1. **Study Daily** - 1-2 hours per day, not all at once
2. **Practice Coding** - Type every example
3. **Complete Homework** - Required for next session
4. **Ask for Help** - Use Slack, don't wait until Sunday
5. **Review Notes** - Reinforce learning

### For Final Project:
1. **Start Planning Early** - Don't wait until Sunday 4
2. **Keep It Simple** - Demonstrate skills, don't over-engineer
3. **Test As You Go** - Don't write everything then test
4. **Document Daily** - Update README as you progress
5. **Ask for Feedback** - Mentors are here to help
