## Answer Key

### Section 1: Multiple Choice

1. **B** - Context
   - *Explanation: CRAFT = Context, Role, Action, Format, Tone.*

2. **B** - "Write a professional email to my manager requesting vacation time..."
   - *Explanation: Specific prompts with context and constraints produce better results.*

3. **B** - Verify the claim with reliable sources
   - *Explanation: AI can hallucinate statistics; always verify factual claims.*

4. **C** - Customer social security numbers and personal data
   - *Explanation: Never share sensitive PII with public AI tools.*

5. **B** - To refine the output by adjusting instructions based on results
   - *Explanation: Iteration improves results through progressive refinement.*

6. **B** - Provide an example of your brand voice in the prompt
   - *Explanation: Examples help AI match specific styles.*

7. **B** - The document plus who the summary is for and what format you want
   - *Explanation: Context about audience and format improves summaries.*

8. **B** - Claude generally handles longer documents better
   - *Explanation: Claude has a longer context window in many cases.*

9. **B** - Review, edit, and verify before using
   - *Explanation: AI outputs need human review before business use.*

10. **B** - "Give me 5 different approaches to [problem] with pros and cons..."
    - *Explanation: Explicit requests for options generate multiple alternatives.*

### Section 2: True/False

11. **False** - AI statistics can be hallucinated; always verify.

12. **True** - Examples in prompts help AI understand desired output.

13. **False** - Confidential data shouldn't go into public AI tools.

14. **True** - Iterative refinement is a recommended prompting strategy.

15. **False** - Standard AI tools don't access real-time internet data (unless specifically enabled).

### Section 3: Scenario-Based

16. **B** - Rewrite your prompt to be more specific
    - *Explanation: Vague prompts get vague results; specificity improves output.*

17. **B** - Edit the output to match your style guide
    - *Explanation: AI outputs should be edited to meet your standards.*

18. **B** - Anonymize or remove personal information before sharing with AI
    - *Explanation: Protect privacy by anonymizing data before AI analysis.*

19. **B** - "Act as an experienced B2B marketing strategist with expertise in tech companies"
    - *Explanation: Specific, relevant roles produce better results than generic ones.*

20. **B** - Ask AI to "Shorten this to under 100 words while keeping the key message"
    - *Explanation: Iterate by giving specific instructions for improvement.*
