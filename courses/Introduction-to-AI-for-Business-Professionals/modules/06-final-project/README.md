# Module 6: Final Project - AI Opportunity Assessment

## Project Overview

**Objective**: Apply everything you've learned to create a comprehensive AI opportunity assessment for your organization, including documented opportunities, prioritization, risk assessment, and an action plan.

**Duration**: 2-3 hours

**Deliverable**: Complete AI Opportunity Assessment document

---

## Project Context

Throughout this course, you've learned:
- What AI is and how it works (Module 1)
- How to calculate AI ROI and business value (Module 2)
- How to identify and prioritize AI opportunities (Module 3)
- How to assess and manage AI risks (Module 4)
- How to use AI tools effectively (Module 5)

Now you'll combine these skills to create a practical document you can use in your organization.

---

## Project Requirements

Your AI Opportunity Assessment must include:

### Part 1: Executive Summary (1 page)
- Organization context
- Key findings
- Top 3 recommended opportunities
- Estimated impact
- Recommended next steps

### Part 2: AI Opportunity Inventory (3-5 opportunities)
- Detailed documentation for each opportunity
- PAIN framework application
- 4H test results
- Value estimation

### Part 3: Prioritization Analysis
- VALUE framework scoring for all opportunities
- Priority matrix visualization
- Recommended sequence

### Part 4: Risk Assessment
- Risk evaluation for top 3 opportunities
- Mitigation strategies
- Governance recommendations

### Part 5: Action Plan
- 90-day roadmap
- Resource requirements
- Success metrics
- Stakeholder engagement plan

### Part 6: Business Case for #1 Priority
- Full business case with ROI calculation
- Implementation approach
- Risk mitigation
- Success criteria

## Grading Rubric

| Component | Points | Criteria |
|-----------|--------|----------|
| Executive Summary | 10 | Clear, compelling, accurate |
| Opportunity Inventory | 25 | 3-5 well-documented opportunities with analysis |
| Prioritization | 15 | VALUE scoring complete, matrix clear |
| Risk Assessment | 20 | Thorough evaluation with mitigations |
| Action Plan | 15 | Realistic, actionable 90-day roadmap |
| Business Case | 15 | Complete ROI analysis, clear recommendation |
| **Total** | **100** | |

### Grading Standards

**A (90-100)**: Exceptional work. All sections complete with thorough analysis. Clear business value demonstrated. Realistic and actionable recommendations. Could be presented to leadership.

**B (80-89)**: Good work. Most sections complete with solid analysis. Minor gaps in detail or analysis. Recommendations are reasonable.

**C (70-79)**: Satisfactory. Basic requirements met. Some sections incomplete or superficial. Recommendations may lack specificity.

**F (Below 70)**: Incomplete or insufficient. Multiple sections missing or poorly developed. Recommendations not actionable.

## Tips for Success

### Do:
- Use real opportunities from your organization
- Be realistic about data readiness and feasibility
- Consider your organization's culture and readiness
- Quantify value where possible, even if estimates
- Think about quick wins AND strategic opportunities
- Include stakeholder perspectives

### Don't:
- Make up data or unrealistic projections
- Ignore risks or implementation challenges
- Recommend AI for everything
- Forget about people and change management
- Overlook data and privacy concerns

### If You're Stuck:
- Review Module 3 exercises for opportunity ideas
- Use the frameworks (PAIN, 4H, VALUE) systematically
- Start with one opportunity and build from there
- Ask: "What would convince a skeptical executive?"
