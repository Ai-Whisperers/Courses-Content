# Course Syllabus: AI Ethics and Governance

## Course Information

- **Course Title**: AI Ethics and Governance
- **Duration**: 16-20 hours (3-4 weeks, part-time)
- **Format**: Self-paced online with case studies and workshops
- **Level**: All levels (non-technical focus)

---

## Course Description

This course equips leaders, compliance professionals, and decision-makers with the knowledge to implement responsible AI practices. Students learn to identify and mitigate AI bias, navigate regulations (GDPR, EU AI Act), assess risks, and create comprehensive company AI policies. The focus is on practical governance frameworks that protect the organization while enabling innovation. By course completion, participants will have a complete AI governance framework ready for implementation.

---

## Learning Outcomes

Upon successful completion, students will be able to:

1. **Identify AI Ethics Issues** - Recognize bias, fairness, and transparency concerns in AI systems
2. **Navigate Regulations** - Understand GDPR, EU AI Act, and emerging AI legislation
3. **Assess AI Risks** - Evaluate privacy, security, liability, and reputational risks
4. **Create AI Policies** - Develop comprehensive company AI usage guidelines
5. **Build Governance Frameworks** - Implement oversight structures for AI adoption
6. **Manage Compliance** - Ensure AI implementations meet regulatory requirements
7. **Handle IP Concerns** - Address copyright, data ownership, and confidentiality
8. **Lead Change** - Guide organizations through responsible AI adoption

---

## Module Schedule

### Week 1: Foundations

**Module 1: Understanding AI Ethics (4 hours)**
- What makes AI ethical (and unethical)
- Types of AI bias and their sources
- Fairness, accountability, and transparency
- Real-world case studies of AI ethics failures
- Impact on customers, employees, and society
- Ethical frameworks for decision-making

**Module 2: The Regulatory Landscape (4 hours)**
- GDPR and AI implications
- EU AI Act overview and risk categories
- US regulatory developments
- Industry-specific regulations (finance, healthcare)
- Global AI governance trends
- Compliance requirements checklist

### Week 2: Risk & Policy

**Module 3: Risk Assessment & Management (4 hours)**
- AI risk identification framework
- Data privacy and confidentiality risks
- IP and copyright considerations
- Liability and accountability questions
- Reputational risk assessment
- Building risk mitigation strategies

**Module 4: Creating AI Policies (4 hours)**
- Components of an AI usage policy
- Acceptable use guidelines
- Data handling requirements
- Employee training requirements
- Vendor and third-party AI governance
- Workshop: Draft your company's AI policy

### Week 3-4: Implementation

**Module 5: Governance Frameworks (3 hours)**
- AI governance structures
- Roles and responsibilities
- Oversight mechanisms
- Audit and monitoring processes
- Escalation procedures
- Board and executive reporting

**Module 6: Implementation & Change Management (3 hours)**
- Building organizational buy-in
- Training and awareness programs
- Phased rollout strategies
- Measuring governance effectiveness
- Continuous improvement
- Final assessment and action planning

## Success Tips

1. **Think Holistically** - Ethics connects legal, technical, and business concerns
2. **Stay Current** - Regulations evolve rapidly; follow updates
3. **Be Practical** - Focus on implementable policies, not perfection
4. **Engage Stakeholders** - Ethics requires cross-functional input
5. **Document Everything** - Paper trails protect the organization
6. **Start Early** - Governance is easier to build than retrofit
7. **Learn from Others** - Case studies reveal common pitfalls
