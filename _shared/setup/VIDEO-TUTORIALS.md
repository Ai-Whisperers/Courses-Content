## Tips for Video Learning

1. **Watch at 1.5x speed** - Save time on slow explanations
2. **Pause and practice** - Don't just watch, do
3. **Take timestamps** - Note important moments
4. **Code along** - Type what they type
5. **Revisit if stuck** - Videos are rewatchable
