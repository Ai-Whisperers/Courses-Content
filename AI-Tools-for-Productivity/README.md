## Course Description

Transform your daily work with AI-powered productivity tools. This comprehensive full-day workshop teaches employees how to leverage ChatGPT, Claude, and other AI tools to work faster, smarter, and more creatively.

From writing emails to analyzing data, from creating presentations to managing meetings - learn practical AI applications that deliver immediate productivity gains. No technical background required.

## What You'll Learn

### Module 1: AI for Writing & Research (90 min)
- ChatGPT and Claude for content creation
- Writing emails, reports, and documentation
- Research and summarization
- Editing and proofreading
- Overcoming writer's block
- Hands-on: Write a business report with AI

### Module 2: AI for Data Analysis (90 min)
- AI-powered spreadsheet tools
- Data cleaning and preparation
- Creating formulas with AI assistance
- Generating charts and visualizations
- Analyzing trends and patterns
- Hands-on: Analyze sales data with AI

### Module 3: AI for Presentations (60 min)
- AI-assisted slide creation
- Design suggestions and layouts
- Content generation for presentations
- Speaker notes and talking points
- Visual asset creation
- Hands-on: Create a pitch deck with AI

### Module 4: AI for Meetings & Collaboration (60 min)
- AI meeting assistants (recording and transcription)
- Automated meeting notes and summaries
- Action item extraction
- Meeting preparation with AI
- Follow-up email generation
- Hands-on: Process a recorded meeting

### Module 5: AI for Customer Service (60 min)
- AI for customer inquiry responses
- Handling difficult conversations
- Multi-language support
- Quality assurance
- Response templates
- Hands-on: Draft customer responses

### Module 6: AI for Coding Assistance (60 min)
- GitHub Copilot for non-developers
- AI for Excel/Google Sheets formulas
- AI for basic scripting and automation
- No-code AI tools
- Troubleshooting tech issues
- Hands-on: Automate a repetitive task

### Module 7: Security & Privacy (45 min)
- Data privacy best practices
- What NOT to share with AI
- Company policy compliance
- Secure AI tool usage
- GDPR and compliance considerations

### Module 8: Prompt Engineering Basics (45 min)
- Fundamentals of effective prompting
- Structuring clear requests
- Context and instructions
- Iterative refinement
- Building personal prompt libraries
- Hands-on: Master prompt workshop
