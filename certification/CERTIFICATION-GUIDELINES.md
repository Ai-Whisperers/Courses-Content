# Certification Guidelines

## Overview

This document outlines the requirements, processes, and standards for issuing course certifications.

---

## Certification Eligibility

### Minimum Requirements

1. **Attendance/Completion**
   - Self-paced: Complete 100% of course content
   - Instructor-led: Attend minimum 80% of sessions

2. **Assessment Performance**
   - Module quizzes: Minimum 70% average
   - Practical exercises: Complete all required exercises
   - Final assessment: Minimum 70% score

3. **Project Submission** (where applicable)
   - Submit capstone/final project
   - Project meets rubric requirements
   - Minimum passing grade achieved

---

## Assessment Components

### Module Quizzes (30% of final grade)
- Multiple choice and short answer questions
- Test knowledge retention and concept understanding
- Unlimited attempts allowed (highest score counts)

### Practical Exercises (40% of final grade)
- Hands-on application of learned skills
- Evaluated against provided rubrics
- Must complete all required exercises

### Final Project (30% of final grade)
- Comprehensive project applying course concepts
- Evaluated on functionality, quality, and documentation
- Must achieve minimum 70% to pass

---

## Grading Scale

| Score Range | Grade | Certificate Status |
|-------------|-------|-------------------|
| 90-100% | A | Certified with Distinction |
| 80-89% | B | Certified with Merit |
| 70-79% | C | Certified |
| 60-69% | D | Conditional (remediation required) |
| Below 60% | F | Not Certified |

---

## Certificate Types

### 1. Certificate of Completion
- Basic certification for completing course content
- No assessment requirements
- Suitable for awareness-level courses

### 2. Certificate of Proficiency
- Standard certification with assessment
- Requires passing all assessments
- Includes skills validation

### 3. Certificate of Excellence
- Honors certification
- Requires 90%+ overall score
- Includes distinction notation

---

## Certification Process

### Step 1: Course Completion
- Complete all modules and lessons
- Mark all content as complete in LMS

### Step 2: Assessment Submission
- Complete all quizzes
- Submit all practical exercises
- Submit final project (if applicable)

### Step 3: Evaluation
- Automated grading for quizzes
- Instructor evaluation for projects
- Final grade calculation

### Step 4: Certificate Issuance
- Verify all requirements met
- Generate unique certificate ID
- Issue digital certificate
- Send notification to participant

---

## Certificate Format

### Digital Certificate Includes:
- Participant's full name
- Course title and duration
- Completion date
- Final grade/score
- Learning outcomes achieved
- Unique verification code
- QR code for verification
- Digital signatures

### Optional Physical Certificate:
- Premium paper stock
- Official letterhead
- Embossed seal
- Physical signatures

---

## Verification System

### Online Verification
- Public verification portal
- Enter certificate ID to verify
- Display certificate details and status

### QR Code
- Links to verification page
- Mobile-friendly access
- Real-time status check

---

## Certificate Validity

| Course Type | Validity Period | Renewal Requirements |
|-------------|-----------------|---------------------|
| Foundational | Lifetime | None |
| Technical | 2 years | Continuing education |
| Specialized | 1 year | Annual assessment |
| Advanced | 3 years | Project portfolio |

---

## Revocation Policy

Certificates may be revoked for:
- Academic dishonesty
- Fraudulent completion records
- Misrepresentation of credentials
- Violation of terms of service

---

## Appeals Process

1. Submit written appeal within 30 days
2. Provide supporting documentation
3. Review by certification committee
4. Decision within 14 business days
5. Final decision is binding
