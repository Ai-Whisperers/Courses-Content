# Módulo 04: Servicio al Cliente y Personalización

## Objetivos del Módulo
- Implementar sistemas de atención al cliente inteligentes
- Desarrollar estrategias de personalización efectiva
- Integrar IA en customer experience
- Optimizar Net Promoter Score (NPS)

## Contenidos

### Unidad 1: Inteligencia en Servicio al Cliente
- Implementación de chatbots 24/7
- Automated response routing
- Sentiment analysis en feedback
- Crisis management automation

### Unidad 2: Personalización Avanzada
- Customer data platform setup
- Dynamic content personalization
- Predictive customer behavior
- Real-time recommendation engines

### Unidad 3: Multicanal Customer Experience
- WhatsApp Business integration
- Voice bots y IVR automation
- Social media customer care
- Email personalization scaling

### Unidad 4: Analytics de Customer Experience
- Customer journey mapping avanzado
- Touchpoint attribution modeling
- Customer lifetime value prediction
- Churn prevention strategies

### Unidad 5: Ethical AI en Customer Relations
- Privacy compliance en personalization
- Bias detection en customer algorithms
- Transparent AI explanations
- Human-AI collaboration models

## Metodología
- **Chatbot implementation**: 2 bots funcionales para diferentes use cases
- **Personalization campaigns**: 3 campañas hyper-personalized
- **CX analytics setup**: Customer analytics dashboard completo
- **Ethics implementation**: AI governance framework

## Herramientas
- WhatsApp Business API para messaging automation
- ChatGPT API para customer response automation
- Salesforce Service Cloud para CRM integration
- Amplitude/Heap para customer behavior analytics
- Privacy compliance tools (OneTrust)

## Evaluación
- **Customer satisfaction improvement**: 30% (NPS increase achieved)
- **Personalization effectiveness**: 25% (Conversion uplift measured)
- **Automation implementation**: 25% (Functionality y user adoption)
