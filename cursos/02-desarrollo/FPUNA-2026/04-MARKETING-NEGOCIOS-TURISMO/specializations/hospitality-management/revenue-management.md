# Módulo 05: Revenue Management Estratégico

## Objetivos del Módulo
- Dominar técnicas de revenue optimization
- Implementar pricing dinámico inteligente
- Desarrollar forecast preciso de demanda
- Maximizar profitability en hospitality

## Contenidos

### Unidad 1: Revenue Management Fundamentals
- Conceptos básicos de revenue management
- Forecasting techniques y tools
- Elasticidad de precio y demand
- Competitive pricing strategies

### Unidad 2: Pricing Dinámico
- Rule-based pricing automation
- AI-powered dynamic pricing
- Price optimization para diferentes segments
- Seasonality y event-based pricing

### Unidad 3: Inventory Management
- Room/yield management optimization
- Overbooking strategies y risk management
- Channel parity enforcement
- Length-of-stay optimization

### Unidad 4: Distribution Channel Optimization
- OTA (Online Travel Agencies) management
- Direct booking incentives
- Channel profitability analysis
- Partnership revenue optimization

### Unidad 5: Performance Analytics y Reporting
- Revenue per available room (RevPAR) tracking
- Market share analysis
- Competitive intelligence gathering
- Executive dashboard design

## Metodología
- **Forecasting models**: 3 modelos predictivos para demanda turística
- **Pricing optimization**: Automated pricing system implementation
- **Channel management**: OTT revenue optimization plan
- **Analytics dashboard**: Executive reporting system

## Herramientas
- Duetto Research para competitive intelligence
- RateTiger/OTA Insight para pricing optimization
- Tableau/Power BI para revenue dashboards
- Python/R para forecasting models
- API integrations con booking platforms

## Evaluación
- **Forecasting accuracy**: 30% (Prediction vs actual performance)
- **Revenue optimization**: 30% (Improvement in RevPAR/yield)
- **Pricing strategy**: 20% (Dynamic pricing system effectiveness)
- **Analytics excellence**: 20% (Dashboard utility y insights)
