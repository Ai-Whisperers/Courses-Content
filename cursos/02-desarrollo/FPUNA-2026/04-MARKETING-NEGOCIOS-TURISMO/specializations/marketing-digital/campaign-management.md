# Módulo 05: Gestión Estratégica de Campañas

## Objetivos del Módulo
- Aprender planificación estratégica de campañas
- Dominar gestión de presupuesto marketing
- Desarrollar habilidades de medición y optimización
- Crear reportes ejecutivos impactantes

## Contenidos

### Unidad 1: Planificación Estratégica de Campañas
- Brief definition y objetivos SMART
- Audience segmentation avanzada
- Budget allocation lanza estrategias
- Timeline y milestone planning

### Unidad 2: Campaign Execution Excellence
- Cross-platform campaign setup
- Real-time optimization techniques
- Creative development y testing
- Vendor management y collaboration

### Unidad 3: Attribution y Measurement
- Marketing attribution models explained
- Incrementality measurement
- Customer lifecycle value calculation
- Branding impact assessment

### Unidad 4: Dashboard y Reporting Ejecutivo
- Dashboard design principles para executives
- Financial impact reporting
- ROI quantification y justification
- Storytelling con datos marketing

### Unidad 5: Post-Campaign Analysis y Optimization
- Campaign debriefing y learnings
- Performance benchmarking industry
- Budget reallocation recommendations
- Strategy refinement para futuras campañas

## Metodología
- **Campaign planning**: 2 briefs completos para clientes reales
- **Execution management**: Campaign management full-cycle
- **Analytics implementation**: Advanced attribution setup
- **Executive reporting**: Board-level presentations

## Herramientas
- Adobe Analytics o similar para advanced attribution
- Tableau/Power BI para executive dashboards
- MarketingCloud automation platforms
- Google Ads API para budget management
- Attribution modeling tools

## Evaluación
- **Campaign planning**: 25% (Strategy y execution plan quality)
- **Performance delivery**: 25% (Results achieved vs targets)
- **Analytics sophistication**: 25% (Attribution y measurement accuracy)
- **Executive communication**: 25% (Reporting quality y insights)
