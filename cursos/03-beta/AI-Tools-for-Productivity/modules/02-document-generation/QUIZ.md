## Answer Key

### Section 1: Multiple Choice

1. **C** - Define - determine document type, audience, and purpose
   - *Explanation: The workflow starts with defining what you need before crafting prompts or generating content.*

2. **B** - The audience for the document
   - *Explanation: Audience is essential for AI to determine appropriate tone, complexity, and focus.*

3. **B** - The requirements and success metrics
   - *Explanation: These are factual elements that AI may generate inaccurately; they must be verified against actual business needs.*

4. **B** - "As a [user type], I want to [action] so that [benefit]"
   - *Explanation: This is the standard user story format that captures who, what, and why.*

5. **C** - Review and edit to match your voice and verify accuracy
   - *Explanation: AI outputs are drafts that need human review for voice, accuracy, and appropriateness.*

6. **B** - AI does 80% of drafting/structure, you add 20% personal judgment and voice
   - *Explanation: AI handles heavy lifting; you add unique context, judgment, and personal touches.*

7. **C** - Provide examples of your writing style and edit AI outputs
   - *Explanation: Showing examples helps AI match your style, and editing ensures authenticity.*

8. **C** - Specify the target length and ask to reduce while keeping key information
   - *Explanation: Specific instructions produce better results than vague requests.*

9. **C** - When including statistics, facts, or specific claims
   - *Explanation: AI can hallucinate facts and statistics; these must always be verified.*

10. **B** - To provide key conclusions and recommendations that stand alone
    - *Explanation: Executive summaries should allow busy readers to understand main points without reading the full document.*

### Section 2: True/False

11. **False** - AI outputs are starting points that require human review, editing, and verification before use.

12. **True** - More context helps AI generate more relevant and accurate content for your specific situation.

13. **False** - Specific refinement requests ("make the introduction more direct" vs "make it better") produce better results.

14. **False** - AI frequently generates plausible-sounding but inaccurate statistics; all data must be verified.

15. **True** - Iterative refinement with specific feedback consistently produces higher quality outputs.

### Section 3: Scenario-Based

16. **B** - Remove the fabricated metrics and add only the data you actually have
    - *Explanation: Never include fabricated data; use only real metrics from your actual project.*

17. **B** - Edit to match your natural tone while keeping the professional content
    - *Explanation: Maintain your authentic voice while preserving the quality structure and content.*

18. **C** - Use placeholder values and add real pricing after generation
    - *Explanation: Don't input confidential information; use placeholders for sensitive data.*

19. **C** - It's too vague - missing specific action and measurable benefit
    - *Explanation: Good user stories have specific, testable actions and clear, measurable benefits.*

20. **A** - State the actual time including AI assistance
    - *Explanation: Transparency about AI assistance is the ethical approach; acknowledge the tools you used.*
