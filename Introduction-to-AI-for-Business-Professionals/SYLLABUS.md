# Introduction to AI for Business Professionals - Syllabus

## Course Information

**Title:** Introduction to AI for Business Professionals
**Duration:** 4 hours (half-day workshop)
**Format:** In-person or Virtual
**Target Audience:** Executives, managers, non-technical staff
**Level:** Beginner
**Prerequisites:** None - No technical background required

---

## Course Overview

Demystify AI and machine learning for business leaders. This practical, non-technical workshop helps executives and managers understand AI's potential, identify opportunities in their business, and make informed decisions about AI adoption.

---

## Learning Outcomes

By completing this course, participants will be able to:

1. Explain AI and ML concepts without technical jargon
2. Identify 3-5 AI opportunities in their organization
3. Calculate potential ROI for AI initiatives
4. Evaluate AI vendors and solutions effectively
5. Understand risks and create governance frameworks
6. Use AI tools productively in daily work (ChatGPT, Claude)

---

## Course Structure

### Module 1: AI Demystified (60 minutes)
**09:00 - 10:00**

- What is AI/ML - cutting through the buzzwords
- Understanding different types of AI
- How AI actually works (simple explanations)
- Common misconceptions about AI
- Real-world examples across industries

### Module 2: Business Value & ROI (60 minutes)
**10:15 - 11:15** (includes 15-min break)

- Real business use cases across industries
- Calculating ROI for AI initiatives
- Quick wins vs. long-term transformation
- Cost considerations and budgeting
- AI tools landscape overview

### Module 3: Identifying Opportunities & Managing Risk (60 minutes)
**11:15 - 12:15**

- Framework for spotting AI opportunities
- Which business processes benefit most
- Data readiness assessment
- Limitations and failures of AI
- Risks, ethics, and governance

### Module 4: Hands-On with AI Tools (60 minutes)
**12:30 - 13:30** (includes 15-min break)

- Setting up ChatGPT and Claude
- AI for email writing and communication
- AI for reports and presentations
- AI for research and analysis
- Best practices and security
